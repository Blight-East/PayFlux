# How KYC and Underwriting Reviews Work

## Overview

KYC (Know Your Customer) and underwriting reviews verify merchant identity and business behavior.

## Why Reviews Exist

Financial institutions must comply with AML, sanctions, and fraud prevention laws.

## How Reviews Work

Systems validate:

- Legal entity
- Beneficial ownership
- Bank accounts
- Business model
- Transaction behavior

## What Triggers Reviews

- Volume growth
- Business model change
- Geographic shifts
- Compliance audits
- Risk alerts

## Why Reviews Pause Payouts

Funds may be held while documentation is validated.

## What Support Can and Cannot Do

Support cannot bypass regulatory review or compliance rules.

## What Infrastructure Can Do

Infrastructure can:

- Track document requests
- Preserve review timelines
- Centralize compliance data

## Where Payflux Fits

Payflux maintains structured review history across processors.

> [!NOTE]
> Payflux does not override regulatory or underwriting decisions.
