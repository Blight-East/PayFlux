Auditability

Every decision PayFlux makes is traceable, exportable, and designed for processor conversations and compliance documentation.

Evidence Narratives

When PayFlux detects elevated risk, it generates structured evidence narratives that document what was detected, why it matters from the processor's perspective, and what remediation steps are recommended. These narratives are designed to be shared directly with processor risk teams.

Each narrative includes: the triggering signals, their individual weights in the risk score, the processor-specific context for why those signals matter, and recommended remediation actions with expected impact on risk posture.

Decision Transparency

PayFlux uses deterministic scoring specifically to enable full decision transparency. Every risk score includes a complete decision lineage:

  • Signal attribution — Which signals contributed and their individual weights
  • Threshold documentation — What thresholds were applied and why
  • Decision lineage — Full trace from raw event to final risk band
  • Configuration versioning — Which scoring configuration version was active

Export and Compliance

All risk data, evidence narratives, and tier history are exportable in structured formats for compliance documentation and regulatory reporting.

  • Structured exports — CSV and JSON export of risk events, scores, and evidence
  • Compliance reports — Pre-formatted reports aligned with common processor audit requirements
  • API access — RESTful audit log API for programmatic integration with compliance tooling
  • Tier history — Complete history of account tier changes with timestamps and justifications

Built for Processor Conversations

When a processor initiates a risk review, merchants need to respond quickly with structured evidence. PayFlux pre-generates remediation evidence packages that include: current risk posture summary, trend analysis showing improvement trajectory, specific signal breakdowns addressing processor concerns, and documented remediation actions already taken.

This transforms the processor conversation from reactive defense to proactive risk management — demonstrating operational maturity that processors value.
