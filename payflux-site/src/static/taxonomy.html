Processor Classifications

PayFlux categorizes payment processors to apply appropriate risk models and signal calibration.

  • Payment Gateways — Direct payment processing platforms with risk assessment capabilities (Stripe, Adyen, Braintree)
  • Acquirers — Merchant acquirers and payment service providers that underwrite merchant accounts
  • Networks — Card networks and payment rails (Visa, Mastercard, Amex)
  • Alternative Processors — Non-traditional payment methods and emerging platforms (BNPL, crypto, wallets)

Risk Categories

Every risk signal detected by PayFlux is classified into one of five primary categories for structured analysis and reporting.

  • Fraud — Unauthorized transactions, identity misuse, and card-not-present abuse
  • Compliance — Regulatory violations, PCI DSS gaps, and policy breaches
  • Operational — System failures, processing errors, and routing degradation
  • Reputational — Brand risk, customer impact, and chargeback ratio exposure
  • Strategic — Market risk, partnership dependencies, and processor concentration
