Signal Registry

Real-time risk indicators and detection patterns used by PayFlux to surface actionable intelligence from payment processor data.

Velocity Signals

Velocity signals track the rate and frequency of transactions from a single source within defined time windows. Abnormal velocity patterns are among the strongest early indicators of fraud, card testing, or retry storms.

  • Rapid transaction sequences from a single merchant or card hash
  • Threshold breaches against rolling 5-minute, 1-hour, and 24-hour windows
  • Behavioral baseline deviation calculated from 30-day merchant history
  • Retry intensity scoring for soft-decline recovery patterns

Geographic Signals

Geographic signals monitor location-based risk patterns including cross-border activity, impossible travel scenarios, and concentration in high-risk jurisdictions.

  • Geo-velocity mismatches between transaction locations
  • High-risk region concentration exceeding merchant baseline
  • IP-to-billing address inconsistency scoring
  • Cross-border transaction ratio shifts

Behavioral Signals

Behavioral signals detect deviations from normal transaction patterns and merchant behavior, comparing current activity against established baselines.

  • Device fingerprint clustering and anomaly detection
  • User session behavior changes
  • Amount distribution shifts relative to merchant category norms
  • Payment method switching patterns

Network Signals

Network signals identify relationships and connections between transactions, merchants, and actors that individual transaction analysis would miss.

  • Shared infrastructure detection across merchant accounts
  • Ring detection through graph analysis of transaction flows
  • Relationship scoring between seemingly unrelated entities
  • Coordinated timing pattern identification
